About Unibit

An Experiment In Decentralized Organizations And Blockchain Technologies

The Next Step Forward

Our team set out to build an unstoppable application. In the beginning we were limited by the tools available to us, so naturally we opted to develop a framework that would support the applications we dreamed of building. The conceptualization of Unibit was a multi year process which progressed through many sprints, pivots, hackathons and support from our community.

The original vision has evolved into developing the environment for creating an ecosystem of applications. Over time our introspection has allowed us to be agnostic in our worldview of blockchains, and enables us to focus on innovation which will further the impact and utility of these technologies. Ultimately we designed Unibit to be an interoperable layer for the various blockchain networks to integrate unique tools, features and services.
